Creating an Account

1. If you already have an account, this step can be skipped.
2. Click the “Create Account” button located at the bottom of the screen.
3. You will be taken to the account creation screen. Click the “Choose a username” text field to input a username. Click the “Choose a password” text field to input a password. Finally, confirm your password by clicking the “Confirm password” text field and inputting your chosen password a second time.
4. When all three fields are filled out, click the “Create” button.
5. The conditions of successful account creations are the following:
• You will receive an error message if you leave any fields blank. In this case, fill out any empty fields.
• You will receive an error message if your chosen username already exists. If this happens, please choose a different username.
• You will receive an error message if your password and confirm password inputs are different. If this happens, ensure the passwords match.
6. Once your account is created successfully, you will receive a success message and be redirected back to the login screen.

Signing In

1. Input your username and password into their respective fields and click “Sign In.” 2. The conditions of successful logins are the following: • You will receive an error message if your username does not exist or if your password does not match the account. Ensure accuracy of all inputs. • You will receive an error message if the username or password fields are left blank. Ensure all fields are filled out. 3. Once you are signed in successfully, you will be redirected to the Vacation List screen. If this is a new account, your list of vacations will be blank at the moment.

Logging out

1. To log out, click the back arrow on the top left of the Vacation List screen. 2. You will receive a pop-up asking you to confirm. Click “Yes.” 3. You will be returned to the Sign-In screen. You can choose to sign back in, sign in with a different account, or create a new account.

Adding Sample Vacations

1. Click the kebab menu icon on the top right of the screen to open the menu options. Click “Add Sample Vacations.” 2. A list of sample vacations will populate for you to work with.

Searching

1. To perform a search, locate the “Search” button and text field at the top of the Vacation List screen. 2. Type your search query into the text field and click the “Search” button. If the searched term does not exist, the vacation list will return empty. 3. The search function also supports searching for a vacation by its details or excursions, concepts that will both be explained later in the guide. 4. To reset the list to its original state, click the “Search” button while the text field is empty.

Reports

1. To generate a report, click the kebab menu icon on the top right of the screen and then click the “Generate Report” menu option. 2. If you have no vacations on your account, such as a newly created account, you will receive an error message. Add vacations to generate a report. 3. A report will be generated after a few seconds of loading. A report displays your username, a timestamp, the details of your vacations, and the number of excursions each vacation has associated with it. To leave this screen, click the back arrow on the top left.

Viewing a Vacation’s Details

1. To view a vacation’s details, click on a vacation from the Vacation List screen. 2. The Vacation Details screen shows the vacation’s title, the destination, the hotel’s name, and the vacation’s start and end dates. Underneath that information, a list of associated excursions and their dates are displayed. 3. To return to the Vacation List screen, click the back arrow in the top left corner.

Creating and Saving a New Vacation

1. To create a new vacation, click the + button on the bottom right of the Vacation List screen. You will be redirected to an empty Vacation Details screen. 2. On the Vacation Details screen, fill out the details for your vacation. Vacations need a title, destination, hotel name, start date, and end date. • Leaving any field blank will result in an error when trying to save. • You will receive an error if your end date is before your start date. • You will receive an error if your chosen dates overlap with another vacation. 3. Click the kebab menu on the top right and click the “Save” menu option. You will be redirected back to the Vacation List screen, and your new vacation will be displayed. 4. Your newly created vacation will now be included in searches and reports.

Modifying and Saving a Vacation

1. To edit a vacation, click a vacation’s name from the Vacation List screen. 2. Modify the details you would like to change, then click the kebab menu in the top right corner and click the “Save Vacation” menu option. You will be redirected back to the Vacation List screen.

Deleting a Vacation

1. To delete a vacation, click a vacation from the Vacation List screen. If your chosen vacation has excursions associated with it, you will receive an error when trying to delete it. For now, choose a vacation without excursions, such as the Australian Vacation. 2. Click the top right kebab menu and then click the “Delete Vacation” menu option. You will be redirected back to the Vacation List screen, and the Australian vacation (or your chosen vacation) will be deleted.

Sharing Feature

1. To share your vacation’s details, click on the vacation on the Vacation List that you would like to share. 2. Click on the top right kebab menu. Click on the “Share” menu option. 3. Your phone will open a list of apps and contacts that you can share the information with. You can also copy the vacation details to paste wherever you’d like.

Vacation Notifications

1. Ensure that you have notifications enabled in your app settings for the Vacation Planner application. 2. Click the vacation that you would like to receive notifications on from the Vacation List. 3. Optional: To test the notification feature, change the start or end date of your chosen vacation to the current date. 4. Click the top right kebab menu on the Vacation Details screen. Click the “Notify Me” menu option. • You will receive an error message if both the start and end dates have passed. 5. You will receive a confirmation message. If your start and/or end dates have not passed, you will receive a notification on those dates.

Viewing Excursion Details

1. To view an excursion’s details, click a vacation from the Vacation List screen. At the bottom of the Vacation Details screen, if a vacation has excursions, they will be listed underneath the vacation details. Click the excursion you want to view. 2. You will arrive at the Excursion Details page.

Modifying and Saving an Excursion

1. To modify an excursion, go to the Excursion Details screen of the excursion you want to modify, using the steps in the “Viewing Excursion Details” tutorial. 2. Click the field containing the excursion’s name and change it to a new name. 3. If you would like to change the date, click on the current date and set it to a new date. The new excursion date must be between the vacation’s start and end dates. 4. There is a dropdown menu to change the vacation that the excursion is associated with. It is best to ignore this for now because it automatically chose the vacation that you came from. 5. Click on the kebab menu in the top right corner. Click on the “Save Excursion” menu option. 6. You will be redirected back to the Vacation Details screen where the excursion list reflects your changes.

Creating and Saving New Excursions (From Vacations)

1. To create a new excursion for a vacation, click on the vacation that you would like to add a new excursion for on the Vacation List screen. 2. On the Vacation Details screen, click the “Plus” button on the bottom right of the screen. 3. Fill out the details on the Excursion Details page. When coming to the Excursion Details screen from a vacation, the drop-down menu will automatically choose the correct vacation. Remember that the excursion’s date must be between the vacation’s start and end dates. 4. Click the top right kebab menu and then click “Save Excursion.” 5. You will be redirected back the Vacation Details screen and your newly created excursion will be listed.

Creating and Saving New Excursions (From Scratch)

1. To create an excursion without choosing a vacation beforehand, start at the Vacation List screen and click the “Plus” button on the bottom right of the screen. 2. You will be redirected to the blank Vacation Details screen. Once again, click the “Plus” button on the bottom right of the screen. 3. You will be redirected to a blank Excursion Details screen. Fill out the details for the excursion. The drop-down menu will default to the first vacation listed on your Vacation List. This time, you will want to change the vacation on the drop-down menu manually. This is because you did not come to this page from a specific vacation, so you will need to set the vacation yourself. • Remember that the chosen date for the excursion must be between the vacation’s start and end dates. 4. Once your details have been filled out, click the top right kebab menu icon and then click “Save Excursion.” 5. You will be redirected back to the blank Vacation Details screen. Click to back arrow button on the top left to return to the Vacation List screen. 6. From the Vacation List screen, click the vacation that you added a new excursion to. 7. Your newly created excursion is now listed.

Deleting an Excursion

1. To delete an excursion, click the excursion you want to delete from the Vacation Details screen. 2. On the top right corner of the Excursion Details screen, click the kebab menu and click "Delete Excursion.” 3. Upon being redirected back to the Vacation Details screen, your chosen excursion will be deleted from the excursion list.

Excursion Notifications

h1> 1. To enable notifications for an excursion, go to the Excursion Details screen of the excursion you want to be notified of. Click the kebab menu icon on the top right and choose the “Notify” menu option. 2. You will receive a message telling you that notifications have been turned on if the date has not passed. If the date has passed, you will receive an error message. 3. You will receive a phone notification on the date of the excursion.
